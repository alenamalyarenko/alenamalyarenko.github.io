## Biography

I am an environmental scientist dedicated to bridging the gap between climate science and advanced data analytics. My work primarily focuses on global ocean physics and sea ice dynamics, with a specific interest in Antarctic environments like the Ross Sea.

Currently, I am developing coupled weather-fire models using PALM and FDS frameworks, while integrating Machine Learning and Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) to improve wildfire behavior forecasting.
